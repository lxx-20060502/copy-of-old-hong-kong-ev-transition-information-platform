Introduction

This project explores how EV-related public data can be transformed into practical value for different users and service scenarios in Hong Kong. For the EV manufacturer, access to Vehicle Market Statistics can support a clearer understanding of registration trends, fuel-type shifts, and market direction, helping firms improve business planning, product positioning, and demand assessment. For the map service provider, access to EV Charging Network data can strengthen location-based mobility services by improving charger visibility, navigation support, and user convenience in daily travel.
