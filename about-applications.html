Applications

The project is grounded in two concrete applications. The first is market competitiveness analysis. TD provides vehicle market statistics, including monthly private car registrations categorized by fuel type and district, allowing the EV Manufacturer to analyze adoption patterns and evaluate market position. The second is real-time charging navigation. EPD provides public charger data, including charger status, location, connector type, and operator details, allowing the Map Service Provider to guide users to available charging facilities. Together, these two applications show how the same dataspace framework can support both strategic business analysis and real-time digital services.
